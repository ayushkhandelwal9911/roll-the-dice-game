How To Play

  • Two Players can play this game.
  • Each player get chance to roll the dice alternatively.
  • In Ecah move, a player has choice to roll the dice or hold the current score.
  • If dice turns to one then the player will loose the current score and the chance is passed to the second player.
  • If player hold the current score then the current score will be added to the player score.
  • THE PLAYER WHO GET 100 POINTS FIRST WILL WIN THE GAME.
